          Funny Urban Legends

          Urban legends can be as amusing as the funniest jokes. Sometimes, in fact, the distinction between the two types of folklore gets fuzzy. Some of the most hilarious urban legends are, in fact, old or new jokes told convincingly enough to seem true. Does the distinction really matter in the end? Maybe not. The important thing is, they make us laugh.
