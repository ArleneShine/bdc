***Find the 2014 Calendar of Kids' & Family Movies

Here is a list of the new kids and family movies that came out in 2013. Most of the movies included are rated G or PG, but with the recent explosion of PG-13 titles that are aimed at and marketed to families and kids (superhero movies, adventures movies and such), I have included some PG-13 titles for the benefit of parents and families with older kids.

January 2013

  • DINO TIME (Release postponed - new date unknown, PG)

February 2013

  • ESCAPE FROM PLANET EARTH (February 14, PG)

March 2013

  • JACK THE GIANT SLAYER* (March 1, PG-13)
  • OZ THE GREAT AND POWERFUL (March 8, PG)
  • THE CROODS (March 22, PG)
  • G.I. JOE: RETALIATION* (March 29, PG-13)

April 2013

  • JURASSIC PARK 3D* (April 5, PG-13)

May 2013

  • IRON MAN 3* (May 3)
  • STAR TREK INTO DARKNESS* (May 17)
  • EPIC (May 24)

June 2013

  • MAN OF STEEL* (June 14)
  • MONSTERS UNIVERSITY (June 21)

July 2013

  • DESPICABLE ME 2 (July 3)
  • THE LONE RANGER* (July 3, PG-13))
  • THE SMURFS 2 (July 31, PG)

    August 2013

    • DISNEY'S PLANES (August 9)
    • PERCY JACKSON: SEA OF MONSTERS (August 16, PG)
    • ONE DIRECTION 3D CONCERT MOVIE (August 30)

    September 2013

    • CLOUDY WITH A CHANCE OF MEATBALLS 2 (September 27, PG)

    October 2013

     

    November 2013

    • FREE BIRDS (November 1)
    • THOR: THE DARK WORLD* (November 8)
    • THE HUNGER GAMES: CATCHING FIRE* (November 22)
    • FROZEN (November 27)

    December 2013

    • THE HOBBIT: THE DESOLATION OF SMAUG (December 13)
    • WALKING WITH DINOSAURS (December 20)
    • SAVING MR. BANKS* (December 20, PG-13)

    *Some movies listed on this calendar may be rated PG-13. I list all movies that are categorized as family or children's movies. So that parents can be aware, I also list movies that are marketed to kids and that kids will probably be wanting to see (like super hero movies and Twilight).
